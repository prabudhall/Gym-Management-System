Treadmill:

A treadmill is a device generally used for walking, running, or climbing while staying in the same place.Powerfull FitPro Treadmills (Quantity = 10-12)

Elliptical Machine(Cross Trainer):

An elliptical trainer is a stationary exercise machine used to stair climb, walk, or run without causing excessive pressure to the joints, hence decreasing the risk of impact injuries.

Leg Press Machine:

The Leg Press Machine promotes leg development by isolating the muscles that comprise the leg.

Pec deck machine:

Pec deck machines have been designed to isolate and develop the pec (chest) muscles.

Cable Crossover Machine

Counted among the popular fitness-enhancing equipment, the cable crossover machine offers multiple benefits to improve your body fitness.


Recumbent Bikes

Upright bikes provide a great low-impact form of indoor exercise by simulating a road bike but without the need to go outside.

Smith Machine:

The smith machine allows users to perform a wide array of exercises such as squats, bench press, rows, seated overhead press, and drag curl.

Olympic benches for chest and shoulders

An Olympic weight bench is a specially designed bench with an integrated spotting platform for assistance with maximal loads. Olympic benches can come in flat, decline or incline varieties.

Free weights

A barbell is essentially a long bar that serves as a loading platform for weight plates – it’s totally up to you as to which area of the body you choose to target with it.

Dumbell:

Pro series fixed dumbbells ranging from 1 kg to 60 kg each side.
